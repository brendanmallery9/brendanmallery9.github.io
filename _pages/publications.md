1. _Multi-domain Routing in Delay Tolerant Networks_, with Alan Hylton, Jihun Hwang, Mark Ronnenberg, Miguel Opez, Oliver Chiriac, Sriram Gopalakrishnan, and Tatum Rask. Published in the _Proceedings of the IEEE Aerospace Conference_, 2023.
